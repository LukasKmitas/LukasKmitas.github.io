How does the game-play

Beginning

The player starts off with 1 building (Headquarters), you can see only a small area which is only around your base this is called "Fog of war" you either expand by adding buildings or make a unit to go explore it to see more of the map. Left click on that building opens a GUI panel on the right side, from there you can select which building the player would like to select, a ghost of that building shall appear on the mouse position. It would be either red or green which tells if the player is allowed to build. To confirm the building placement you need to right click to place it down.

The player can sell the building to gain money from the GUI panel except the main building (the one the player starts with). The currency is used for creating buildings and Units.
There are 4 building typs:
Refinery - This is how the player gets money it spawns a unit called Harvester after it finishes collecting it will bring back to Refinery building to deposit.
Barracks - This building spawns infantry soldiers, there are multple enititys in that 1 unit.
WarFactory - Can create vehicles such as tanks, buggys.
AirCraft - Can create air units such as helicopters.

Units
There are 3 classes of units:
Infantry
Vehicles
Air

RiflemanSquad - Infantry type, this unit can be created from Barracks building, it has multple entities, it is cheap to create and it fires basic bullets, when the health goes down each enitity will die off.

Harvester - Vehicle type, This unit can be created from the WarFactory building or it can spawn for free by making the refinery building, this unit is not a combat type it will instead search for a discovered resource tile, it will go there and start harvesting, after it finishes it, it will search for a refinery building if it exists and travel to it, when it gets there it will deposit its resources and the player gets currency.

Buggy - Vehicle type, this unit is very good at scouting, its very fast and has a turret that shoots in all directions.

Tank Aurora - Vehicle type, this unit is a support/debuff unit, it has a healing aura that other units can regain health within distance, for enemies it attacks by shooting a emp wave, it has 2 phases first the charge up, it charges eletricity at its front after fully charged it shoots a wave that gradually expands, enemies that are hit starts to slow down until they stop and they cant move for few seconds.

HammerHead - Air type, this unit has 2 guns on each side of its wing, it shoots in a wide area having poor accuracy but high damage in return.

FireHawk - Air type, this unit shoots 4 rockets it shoots in 2 phases the first 2 first immediately and the last 2 shortly after, it deals very high damage.

Enemy

The enemy creates either a building within its view or a unit every few seconds, it goes through 3 phases:
Exploring: The enemy sends its units to explore the map
Grouping: It will start to group some units making a small army
Attacking: As the name suggests the enemy will start to attack the player for a certain duration

Victory Conditions
To win in this game you need to destroy all the enemy buildings and you lose if the player bases are destroyed.
After the game ends it will show you some stats how many unit/buildings both player and enemy has created and how long the match went for.
